CS4752 Assignment 2 Alpha-Beta searchtree to play connect-4

Execute A2_main.py to play game. In A2_main.py edit P1Player or P2Player(located at line 15 and 16) to change AI depth limit and timelimit.
Edit line 33 to change which player goes first. Executing A2_test.py will run a tournament with the solution.
